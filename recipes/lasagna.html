LASAGNA RECIPE

To the next recipe, we will need the following ingredients

Ingredients:

  • Meat
  • Onion and Garlic
  • Tomato products
  • Sugar
  • Lasagna noodles
  • Cheeses
  • Egg

Process:

  1. Make the meat sauce.
  2. Cook the noodles.
  3. Make the ricotta mixture.
  4. Layer the lasagna.
  5. Cook with foil and bake.
  6. Let the lasagna rest before serving.
